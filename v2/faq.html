You've got Q's, we've got A's

What is different/interesting/new about your business?

We are creating a new path how to realize ideas and giving each individual the power to connect with the crowd, that has the interests, knowledge to make it happen. Each participant can be involved into every creative activity in real-time! They can influence the outcome of the idea. Users can gather feedback,gain social capital, improve their skills, expand their knowledge and be financially rewarded.

Who are your competitors? What differentiates you?

What differentiates us, is that we develop our projects from start to the end. We are creating a new path how to realize ideas. We are giving each individual the power to connect with the crowd, that has the interests and the knowledge to make it happen. We are creating a tool, which enables to contribute more richly, deeply and efficiently than it is possible today. People can participate in every creative activity of the process, that interests you. Our tool enables users to participate in creative projects and connect with the crowd to exchange informations, gather feedback and build things they are passionate about. Each participant can be involved into every creative activity in real-time around the world! They can influence the outcome of the idea and make a real and lasting impact in the world around you. Users can gather feedback, gain social capital, improve their skills and expand their knowledge.

What are your commitments to the startup?

Our commitment is to build something on our own. Take our careers into our own hands and what’s the most important thing, make an impact to the world around us. With our startup we don’t want to create something for ourselves but we want also give this to the people, so they can be part of something big. Where they can communicate, connect, work and realize ideas, which will make an impact in the world around us.

Describe the problem it's solving for whom.

The creative world is one of the most disorganized communities on the planet. The creative community makes our lives interesting; they create stuff that compels us to understand the world, make sense of events, and take action. Yet they often say that their careers are at the mercy of circumstantial opportunities and networks of people they just happen to know, rather than pro actively pushing their careers.

When you starting a new venture, you’re unplugging from the reward system that we’ve all grown up with it. Even if you already have an company and what to create a new product or invent something new, you are always in the risk, what people are most afraid of it. And instead spending millions on marketing research and creating the best solutions, we want to create a tool, where ideas are build by the people and for the people. Everyone can be part of it, the people with knowledge, dreams, passions, ideas, etc. Everything what they just need to do is to be the part of this process we are creating.
